Rice!

Rice is an incredible food that can be filling and healthy if mixed with the right ingredients

Brown rice and White rice are both abundant in carbs, but brown rice has "good carbs" which aren't as unhealthy as most people think. White rice undergoes a process that strips it of its fibers, vitamins, and minerals so it isn't as nutritious as brown rice.

Brown rice is very nutrient rich, it contains:
Fiber
Antioxidants
Vitamins
Minerals

Cereals

The easiest way to eat breakfast without spending 8$ a day. Be careful, many cereals are sweets in disguise!

The most widely available, healthy, and most delicious cereals are the original “Kellogg's corn flakes”, “Cheerios”, and “Fiber One” cereals. They have low sugar, high fiber, high protein, and organic ingredients.
