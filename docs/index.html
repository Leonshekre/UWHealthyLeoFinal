Staying Healthy & Productive In UW!

It's hard to stay healthy in College, especially with Covid still looming about. Eating at the dining halls is costly, boring, and requires timely precision because of how early they close, but fear not!

My goal shall be to provide you with the easiest, most delicious, and cheapest ways to stay healthy in College (with emphasis on ease). I will explain simple recipes, cooking, and nutrition in order to show you that it's not as hard as it seems! I will also research other topics about college that can be very helpful for the real world such as: banking, finance, exercise, office 365, coding, and more! These will contribute to one's physical and mental health by keeping you healthy and productive during your journey through College.
