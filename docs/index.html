Staying Healthy in UW!

It's hard to stay healthy in College, especially with Covid. Eating at the dining halls is costly, boring, and it even closes early, but fear not! My goal shall be to provide you with the easiest, most delicious, and cheapest ways to stay healthy in College (with emphasis on ease).
I will explain cooking, nutrition, exercise, and more to show that it's not as hard as it seems!

Website was built from scratch, this project will continue to be a work in progress for many months, or even years!
